# Overall Research Notes

Across these resources, Kevin Indig focuses on the operational and measurement challenges created by AI-driven search. His work connects AI Overviews, AI Mode, prompt tracking, third-party authority, citations, technical accessibility, and organic growth strategy.

Several recurring themes stand out: AI Search visibility is measurable but not equivalent to rank tracking; AI-powered search systems behave differently across engines and product surfaces; authority signals are topic-specific; and content strategy must adapt to answer-rich search environments where users may compare, read, or accept answers without following classic click patterns.

Kevin Indig is an important expert for this repository because he combines AI Search research, technical SEO, SaaS growth, and content strategy. His perspective helps translate AI-powered SEO from broad speculation into research-backed workflows for measurement, content planning, authority building, and organic growth.

# Key Takeaways for This Repository

- Measure AI visibility separately from classic rankings.
- Segment analysis by AI surface, including AI Overviews, AI Mode, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other engines.
- Build topic-specific authority signals instead of generic off-site mentions.
- Use AI to accelerate SEO workflows while keeping strategy grounded in verified data and human judgment.
- Connect content production to organic growth, technical accessibility, user behavior, and business outcomes.
