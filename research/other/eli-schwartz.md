## Overall Research Notes

Across these resources, Eli Schwartz presents SEO as a product-led and growth-led discipline. His work repeatedly emphasizes that SEO should begin with customers, product value, market opportunity, and business outcomes rather than generic keyword execution or checklist-based tactics.

Several recurring themes stand out: Product-Led SEO is a strategic alternative to playbook-driven SEO; organic traffic should be evaluated by its ability to drive leads, sales, and growth; B2B and B2C SEO programs require audience understanding and scalable execution; international SEO requires market-specific strategy; and AI belongs inside research, content planning, execution, and reporting workflows rather than replacing strategic thinking.

Eli Schwartz is an important expert for this repository because he connects product-led SEO, AI-powered SEO education, organic growth, content strategy, international SEO, B2B SaaS SEO, AEO, and growth marketing. His perspective helps keep AI-powered SEO content production focused on business impact rather than publishing volume.

## Key Takeaways for This Repository

- Start AI-assisted SEO workflows with customer research, product fit, and growth goals.
- Treat content production as one part of a product-led organic growth system, not the whole strategy.
- Use AI to support keyword research, content planning, technical checks, localization planning, and reporting.
- Prioritize topics and pages that can drive qualified demand, leads, sales, or product adoption.
- Build international SEO workflows around local markets, search behavior, and customer needs rather than direct translation.
- Avoid generic SEO playbooks; use AI to improve strategic decision-making, experimentation, and execution quality.
