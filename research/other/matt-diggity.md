# SEO Testing: The Secret To Maximize Your Website Traffic and Profits

- Date: February 7, 2021
- Source: Diggity Marketing
- URL: https://diggitymarketing.com/seo-testing/
- Content Type: Official website article
- Main Topic: SEO testing, experiments, control groups, ranking signals, and traffic growth

## Summary

Matt Diggity explains why SEO requires testing rather than relying only on theory or generic best practices. The article covers experimental and control groups, the challenge of isolating variables, the need for enough test cases, and how testing can reveal tactics before they become widely known.

## Key Insights

- Use experiments to validate whether SEO changes actually affect rankings or traffic.

- Track details carefully so unexpected ranking changes can be investigated.
- Treat SEO testing as a way to make decisions with evidence instead of assumptions.

# Overall Research Notes

Across these resources, Matt Diggity's public work emphasizes practical, test-driven SEO systems that connect content, links, authority, distribution, and measurable business outcomes. His recent official case studies are especially relevant because they address AI search directly: AI referral traffic, AI Overview visibility, Reddit as an AI discovery source, digital PR as third-party validation, and content repurposing across platforms.

The strongest pattern is that AI-powered SEO content production should not be reduced to generating more articles. Diggity's recent resources suggest a broader operating model: identify where audiences search, repurpose proven content into the formats those channels need, structure content clearly, build external validation through PR and communities, and measure AI visibility separately from traditional rankings.

# Key Takeaways for This Repository

- Use AI to scale production workflows, but anchor the system in verified sources, topical authority, and measurable business outcomes.
- Repurpose existing high-quality content across platforms before creating large volumes of net-new content.
- Include Reddit, digital PR, backlinks, and third-party mentions as part of AI search visibility strategy.
- Add structured data, multimodal support, and AI referral tracking to the content production workflow.
- Test AI-powered SEO changes with controlled experiments instead of relying on assumptions.
