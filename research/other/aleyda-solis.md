# Overall Research Notes

Aleyda Solis's recent official resources are unusually strong for this repository because they translate AI search into practical frameworks: checklists, prompt libraries, measurement layers, brand characteristics, market patterns, ecommerce citation analysis, and SEO-vs-GEO comparisons. The recurring pattern is not "publish more with AI," but "measure better, structure better, localize better, and connect optimization to business impact."

Her work also connects AI search with traditional SEO foundations. Technical crawlability, content strategy, ecommerce information architecture, international market behavior, and brand visibility all remain important. AI search changes the discovery environment, but it does not remove the need for accurate research, strong information architecture, and credible content.

# Key Takeaways for This Repository

- Build representative prompt libraries before measuring AI visibility.
- Separate AI search presence, readiness, and business impact in reporting.
- Optimize for AI search with checklists and worksheets that teams can execute.
- Localize AI search strategy by market, vertical, and search behavior.
- Treat brand clarity, technical accessibility, and content usefulness as core AI search inputs.
