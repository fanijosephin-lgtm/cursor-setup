* Published Date: May 30, 2025
* Channel: 97th Floor
* URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wespWY7M-fI
* Duration: 31:52

## Summary

The public YouTube metadata says this video covers Kevin Indig's research into how users interact with Google's AI Overviews. The public description says Kevin's team tracked 70 users across eight search tasks, captured 29 hours of think-aloud sessions, and coded more than 400 AI Overview encounters to study scroll depth, click behavior, emotional reactions, and trust signals.

## Key Insights

* Study how users actually behave in AI search experiences instead of assuming classic SERP behavior still applies.
* Treat AI Overviews as a visibility and trust environment, not only a traffic source.
* Use behavioral research to decide which content formats, query types, and trust signals matter.
* Update SEO and content strategy for search sessions where users may read, compare, and decide without clicking traditional blue links.
