# Overall Research Notes

Across these videos, Eli Schwartz consistently frames SEO as a growth discipline rather than a collection of tactics. His public YouTube appearances connect product-led SEO, international SEO, enterprise SEO, growth marketing, and content strategy around the same principle: organic growth should begin with customers, market opportunity, product value, and measurable business outcomes.

Several themes repeat across the selected videos: do not copy generic playbooks; use SEO to acquire qualified customers; connect content strategy to product and growth; treat international SEO as market expansion rather than translation; and design enterprise SEO around implementation systems. Complete transcripts were not publicly verified, so the summaries above avoid quotation-level claims and stay tied to public metadata, descriptions, and Eli Schwartz's official videos page.

# Key Takeaways for This Repository

* Build AI-assisted SEO workflows around product value, customer needs, and growth goals.
* Use AI for research, clustering, localization planning, reporting, and brief creation, but avoid generic content scaling detached from strategy.
* Treat B2B SaaS SEO as a product-led acquisition system with measurable business outcomes.
* Design international SEO workflows around local market demand, not only translated keywords.
* Keep enterprise SEO recommendations tied to organizational execution, prioritization, and revenue impact.
