# Overall Research Notes

Across these videos, Aleyda Solis presents AI search as an evolution of SEO that requires strong technical foundations, strategy, measurement, and execution. Her YouTube appearances repeatedly connect AI visibility with crawlability, traditional search fundamentals, content strategy, and business reporting.

The recurring theme is that AI-powered SEO should not be reduced to prompt tricks or mass content production. Aleyda's work points toward a more mature workflow: understand the search interface, make content technically accessible, structure content around user needs, measure AI visibility carefully, and prioritize recommendations that teams can actually execute.

# Key Takeaways for This Repository

* Treat crawlability, rendering, internal linking, and indexability as prerequisites for AI-powered SEO content.
* Separate traditional search, AI Mode, AI Overviews, and ecommerce discovery when planning content.
* Use AI to support audits, research, and production, but keep human prioritization and strategy.
* Build reporting that connects AI visibility to business impact without overclaiming attribution.
* Prioritize content strategy and execution systems before scaling AI-assisted publishing.
