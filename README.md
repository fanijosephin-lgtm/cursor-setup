## Research Project

### Topic

AI-powered SEO Content Production

### Research Objective

This project aims to research how leading SEO practitioners use artificial intelligence to improve content production, optimization, semantic search, and scalable SEO workflows for modern B2B SaaS companies. The collected research will as the foundation for building a practical AI-powered SEO playbook in future project stages.

## Research Methodology

Research materials were collected from publicly available sources, including:

1. Official websites
2. LinkedIn
3. YouTube
4. Podcasts
5. Conference presentations
6. Industry blogs
7. Webinar recordings

Whenever information could not be publicly verified (such as unavalaible LinkedIn posts or YouTube transcripts), this was explicitly document instead of making assumptions.

The repository prioritizes research quality over quantity, following the assignment guideline that **high-signal sources are more valuable than large volumes of generic content.**
